Local Taekwondo Championship

Date: December 12, 2024

Location: Community Sports Hall

The Local Taekwondo Championship is set to showcase the best taekwondo talent from around the community. Participants will demonstrate their martial arts skills, discipline, and sportsmanship in an exciting and competitive environment. This event is open to all, from beginners to seasoned practitioners.

Come and witness high-level taekwondo action, and support your favorite athletes. Tickets and event merchandise will be available for purchase on the day of the event.
